A Gentrification Tax for Toronto

When selling their houses, local homeowners profit from the collective efforts of every member of the community. To overturn the pattern, Toronto needs a mechanism to channel the profit generated by land value increase into the communities who created it, and from whom this value is being taken. A gentrification tax would act as a levy on the increase in a propertyâ€™s value which would be exclusively directed to fund locally managed deeply affordable housing.
